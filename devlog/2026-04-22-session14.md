## Theory Additions

### Stress and ease responses (Enneagram-inspired)

Added to `THEORY.md` Relationships section, between Receiver and Evil Twin:

- Under stress an archetype imperfectly does its Receiver's work (Enneagram disintegration parallel)
- Under ease it imperfectly does its Supplier's work (Enneagram integration parallel)
- The specific mood/vibe of each stress or ease response is pair-specific; cells left as future work in the edge table

Added two new columns (`supplier stress`, `receiver ease`) to the edge table with `—` for Supplier/Receiver rows and `n/a` for the 9 cross-triangle pairs, with a note that these are filled by theory track item 0c.

### Natural preference correction

Initial draft said natural archetype is revealed "in the context where you are most at ease." User corrected: natural preference appears at routine/middling stress, not relaxation. Extra stress (triggers Receiver reach) and extra relaxation (triggers Supplier drift) are both deviations. Fixed in THEORY.md Natural Preference sub-section and DESIGN.md Principle 8.

### Context-aware test runs

Added to `THEORY.md`: "Contextual Archetypal Expression" section explaining that axes describe motivational orientation toward situations, context makes measurement explicit, and multi-context data is the cleanest path to natural preference (routine stress context = most stable archetype). Relationship network as corroborating evidence.

Added to `DESIGN.md`: Principle 8, "Archetypal expression is context-specific." Key content: multi-track IS the core feature for multi-context users; relationship data is categorically more sensitive than scores and must never leave logbook in any form.

Added to `DATAMODEL.md`: "Test Contexts and Relationships" section with `quadrantology_contexts` localStorage schema (context-v1 objects with id, label, description, relationships array), format_version 2 run record extension (context_id, context_snapshot, archetype_distribution), and inheritance flow documentation.

Added to `VOCAB.md`: six new terms — stress response, ease response, test context, relationship set, archetype distribution, multi-track.

### AUDIT.md vocabulary fix

Updated archetype pairs table for S4: "Frenemy" → "Evil Twin", "Evil Triplet" → "Supplier/Receiver". These were stale labels from before Session 13 terminology cleanup.
