## Key Theory Decisions

### The master framework
Death and Taxes 2x2: Y = Temporal Horizon (Death↔Life), X = Scarcity Structure (Taxes↔Play). Both axes describe motivational orientation, not just environment. The archetype sits where its two orientations intersect.

### Archetype placements (why, not just where)
- **Hacker/Investigator (Life/Play):** Process-oriented, no terminal goal, game is the point. H leans Life (survival urgency), I leans Play (aesthetic pleasure of inquiry).
- **Contrarian (Life/Taxes):** Life-seeking but tax-sensitive. Friction is the stimulus. Consequentialist: ends justify means. Praxis player, not theorist.
- **Operator (Death/Play):** Mortality-aware but plays the game skillfully. Knows it's rigged, plays to win anyway. Praxis player. Goals of winning, not just playing well.
- **Legalist/Holy Warrior (Death/Taxes):** The generative hinge, not the dark quadrant. Both are order-making theorists/architects. Legalist: inventive, faces forward, paves cowpaths (Minkowski to Einstein). Holy Warrior: restorative, faces backward, recovers lost purity. Both vita contemplativa — structurally dependent on doers.

### Comic/tragic character
Exit triangle = comic (arc bends toward new life, failure modes are comic). Voice triangle = tragic (carries accumulated scar tissue, reform may fail). Voice is more painful because it deals with aged systems and baggage; Exit by definition walks away.

### Philosopher/Doer distinction
Legalist and Holy Warrior are pure vita contemplativa. The four doers can function without a philosopher partner. Philosophers cannot function at all without the cycles doers create. Unpartnered philosophers default to Acting Dead (Bruce Sterling's sense) — structural condition, not pathology.

### Smooth/striated
Smooth space (D&G, in this model) ≠ flatness or ease. It is wilderness, or striated space navigated by someone playing by more fundamental rules (nature, logic, physics) while indifferent to in-game stakes. Hacker = paradigm case. Understands striation as well as or better than Investigator, but operates beneath it. Nakatomi space.

Investigator lives in striated space and experiences no friction through perfect alignment with the institutional grain — not because they're in smooth space.

Contrarian and Operator cannot access unconscious flow. They grapple with realities skill cannot mitigate. They can only choose to mourn or exult in the entropy they're creating.

Legalist and Holy Warrior cannot access flow at all. They ARE the friction — standing at the collision point of smooth and striated.

### Second law
The whole model operates against thermodynamic inevitability. Entropy always increases at system level. No archetype wins against it. H/I are most deluded (narrow neighborhood). C/O are most honest (see whole system, mourn or exult). L/HW are most conscientious (try hardest to slow it, but entropy finds a way through even the most careful architecture).

### MBTI scope
MBTI only measures the consequentialist dimension. Maps cleanly to Life/Taxes (Contrarian, NTs/SPs) and Death/Play (Operator, SJs/NFs). Breaks down at both extremes: Life/Play (too granular — pure process) and Death/Taxes (too abstract — pure philosophy).

### Legalist as Minkowski
The Legalist doesn't create new structure — they formalize structure already latent in the Hacker/Contrarian moves. Paves the cowpaths. The creativity happened upstream.
