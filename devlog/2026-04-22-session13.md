## Key Decisions

- **Evil Triplet abandoned entirely** — the term added nothing over Supplier/Receiver, created confusion with Evil Twin. "You're on the same side" is adequately captured by the E/V coordinate.
- **Frenemy abandoned entirely** — Evil Twin is unambiguous and formally defined; Frenemy was always an informal alias with no additional meaning.
- **Frustrator named** — the third cross-triangle relation type (k=+1 composed with mirror) is now formally named. Dynamic: mutual broadcast, both trying to give, talking past each other.
- **Nemesis/Frustrator dynamics distinguished** — Nemesis = mutual deadlock (both expecting to receive); Frustrator = mutual broadcast (both trying to give). These are structurally opposite failure modes.
