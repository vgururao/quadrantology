## E001 · 2009-03 → 2009-10 · intensity: 8
**NDA Graduation, the Posting**

I graduated from the National Defense Academy and received my initial posting in Yokosuka. The academy had taught me the theory of naval logistics. The posting taught me the gap: theory assumed adequate documentation, a complete inventory record, and compliant upstream supply. The actual fleet had all three problems and a leadership culture that worked around them efficiently and chronically. I spent the first months learning which workarounds were safe and which would fail at the worst possible time.
